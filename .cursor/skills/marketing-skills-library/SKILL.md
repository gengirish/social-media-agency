# Marketing & SEO Skills Library (kostja94)

160+ markdown skills for AI agents covering SEO, content strategy, page types, paid ads, channels, and growth strategies. Works with Cursor and other AI agents.

## Skill Categories

- **SEO** (`seo/`): Technical SEO, on-page optimization, robots.txt, sitemaps, schema markup, title tags, meta descriptions, internal linking
- **Content** (`content/`): Blog posts, case studies, whitepapers, newsletters, social media content
- **Pages** (`pages/`): 40+ page type templates - landing pages, pricing pages, about pages, comparison pages, etc.
- **Paid Ads** (`paid-ads/`): Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, campaign structures
- **Channels** (`channels/`): Email, social media, video, podcast, community channels
- **Strategies** (`strategies/`): Growth strategies, content strategies, launch playbooks
- **Analytics** (`analytics/`): Tracking, attribution, reporting, conversion optimization
- **Components** (`components/`): Reusable UI/UX patterns for marketing pages
- **Platforms** (`platforms/`): Platform-specific optimization guides

## Usage

Reference specific skills when working on marketing tasks. The agent will automatically pick the right skill based on your prompt context.
